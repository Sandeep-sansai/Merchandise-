Add Images Place your slideshow images in the src/assets folder. Update the slides array in home.component.ts with your image paths and captions. How It Works 1. Navbar: Always displayed at the top with a consistent look. 2. Slideshow: Cycles through images with captions. Users can navigate using the “previous” and “next” buttons. 3. Hero Button: Prominently placed button to guide users to book tickets. 4. Footer: Displays quick links to social media or other useful pages. 5. Responsive Design: The layout is mobile-friendly and adapts to various screen sizes. Let me know if you need any enhancements!
